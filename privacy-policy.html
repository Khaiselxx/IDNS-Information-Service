1. Introduction

Welcome to IDNS Information Services' Privacy Policy. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us.

1.1 Who We Are

IDNS Information Services ("IDNS", "we", "us", "our") is a telecommunications and IT services provider committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name, email address, phone number (when you apply for services)
  • Billing and payment information
  • Service address and location data
  • Account credentials (username, password)

2.2 Non-Personal Information

  • IP address and browser information
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Cookies and usage analytics

We only collect information necessary to provide our internet services and improve your experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process service applications and activations
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Process billing and payments
  • Improve our services and website
  • Send service updates and promotional offers (with opt-out option)
  • Comply with legal requirements

4. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share information with:

Recipient Purpose
Service Providers Billing, network maintenance
Law Enforcement Legal compliance only
Business Partners Service delivery (with consent)

5. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request data removal (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt-out: Stop marketing communications

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure servers and firewalls
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring

7. Cookies & Tracking

Learn more about our cookie usage in our Cookie Policy.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
