1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us remember your preferences, analyze usage, and improve your experience.

2. Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Website functionality, navigation Session
Analytics Usage statistics, performance 2 years
Preferences Your choices and settings 1 year
Marketing Personalized ads and content 90 days

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie banner.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use trusted third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics
  • Facebook Pixel: Social media tracking
  • Font Awesome: Icons and graphics

4. Managing Cookies

  • Browser settings (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.)
  • Cookie banner preferences
  • Privacy extensions and tools

5. Cookie Banner

Our cookie banner appears on first visit. You can:

  • Accept All: Enable all cookies
  • Reject: Disable non-essential cookies
  • Manage Preferences: Customize settings

6. Your Rights

  • Opt-out of non-essential cookies
  • Delete cookies anytime
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Browser "Do Not Track" support
