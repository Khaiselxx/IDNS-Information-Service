Our Story

Founded in 2020

IDNS Information Services was established in February 2020 as a System Integrator and IT Solutions Provider, offering system integration, IT products supply, equipment, and technical support for businesses and individuals.

Company Establishment

IDNS Information Services was established in February 2020 as a System Integrator and IT Solutions Provider, offering a wide range of services including system integration, supply of IT products and equipment, and technical support for businesses and individuals.

Pandemic Pivot

At the onset of the global pandemic, the company quickly adapted to the changing environment by expanding its services to include online technical tutorials and remote support solutions. These initiatives were designed to help clients transition smoothly into the new normal, where work-from-home setups and digital operations became essential.

IDNS Internet Launch

As the demand for reliable internet connectivity surged during this period, IDNS Information Services recognized a critical need in the market. This led to the establishment of IDNS Internet, a dedicated service focused on providing fast, stable, and affordable internet connectivity to residential and business clients.

Our Commitment

From humble beginnings, IDNS has continuously evolved by integrating advanced technologies, improving service delivery, and expanding network infrastructure. Today, we remain committed to delivering innovative IT solutions, high-quality internet services, and customer-focused support—empowering communities and businesses to stay connected and productive.
